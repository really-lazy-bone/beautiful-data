Flickr - A Picture is Worth a Single Word

Project Description

Using geolocation and tag (keyword) data for photos taken and uploaded to Flickr during 2013 we aim to map the most commonly tagged words in the US in an interactive data map that features both time-series (month by month) and granularity (zoom) functionality.
